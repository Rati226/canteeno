"A canteen is a store that sells food and drink at an institution like a camp, college, or military base. A canteen can also be a small container used to carry water to drink."

Key points

  • Talk with your child about healthy canteen foods and drinks.
  • canteen options include multigrain sandwiches, soups, pasta, curries, fruit salads and yoghurt.
  • Unhealthy options include deep-fried foods, pastries, cakes, lollies, chips and sugary drinks.

    • Choosing healthy food from the canteen
    Here are some healthy canteen options and ideas. If they’re available at your COLLEGE canteen, these are the options to go for.

    Sandwiches, rolls and wraps

    Encourage your child to choose a multigrain, wholemeal or high-fibre white sandwich, roll or wrap. Healthy fillings include salad and lean chicken or beef, fish, eggs or lentil patties.

    Cold Food

    Your canteen might serve fresh salads, sushi or rice paper rolls – yum!
